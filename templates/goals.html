How ProgressSDG Works

ProgressSDG is a tool to track completion of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) from the United Nations.

There are 16 SDGs covering all kinds of environmental, social, and governance priorities. The 17th SDG measures partnerships required for completing the other 16 goals.
